Come on down, let's explore together

Here at your Community Science museum, you can explore life.

You can explore the past, the present and even parts of the future.

Travel to the universe, or see the life of a single seed grow. When the museum got built, we invited down Morgan Freeman to plant our own tree that is located in the center of our museum.

And "The tree of life" is growing strong. Drop by and see if the tree have enough water, oxygen and light. Have a look at the display by the tree and you can see when the first root came, and how many it is grown.

Long day?

Stop by our museum cafe to enjoy some fresh toast, some healthy local grown fruits or maybe a hot cup of coffee?

We also have some healthy snacks for the smallest. It is room enough for a stroller by the table, and a bottle heater near our drink dispensers.

Children with lots of energy?

In the museum playground the youngest will not only get out the extra energy, but they will also learn

Our staff and volunteers is always around, making sure the next generation is kept safe in the playground
