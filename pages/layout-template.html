Come down, let's explore together

Here at your Community Science museum, you can explore life.

You can explore the past, the present and even parts of the future.

Travel to the universe, or see the life of a single seed grow. When the museum got built, we invited down Morgan Freeman to plant our own tree that is located in the center of our museum.

And "The tree of life" is growing strong. Drop by and see if the tree have enough water, oxygen and light. Have a look at the display by the tree and you can see when the first root came, and how many it is grown.
