Museum cafe

If the day is long, or the kids get hungry. Take the trip down top our museum cafe. Enjoy fresh bread from a local baker and som hot coffee

We also have a range of food aimed at the children. You will always find something to eat, snack or drink.

We also have a bottle warmer for the youngest. It is located next to the soda dispenser

Museum gift shop

On your way out you can stop by our gift shop and bring some cool and interesting things to remind you about your stay.

We have loads of boardgames, experiment sets and some cool science T-shirts.

Accessibility

 Our museum have ramps for wheelchair users, all our display cards have sign language print, and the option to listen to the text.

 We also have a breastfeeding room so you can be in a quiet environment.

 We also want to inform you that smoking is not allowed around the museum.

 We have designated smoking rooms outside the museum. Juts follow the signs.
